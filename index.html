About Us

Welcome to Rug Wrangler, a unique youthful entrepreneurship journey, nestled in the heart of Cochrane, Alberta. Our story begins with a partnership between a young entrepreneur, Eli Cloutier, and his mother-in-law, Michelle Sauvageau.

Eli, an emerging entrepreneur, started his first business at the age of 9, founding a firewood selling company. With a keen sense for business and a relentless drive, he re-invested the money he made with firewood to ventured into the world of gourmet treats, launching a caramel treats venture at 10 years old. Each endeavor not only added to his experience but also to his aspiration to become a better entrepreneur.

At 12, Eli, together with Michelle, founded Rug Wrangler. Our company is built on the foundation of Eli's youthful energy and Michelle's strategic support, creating a dynamic duo that is both ambitious and grounded.

Our mission is to help reduce the cost of living and working in a clean and healthy environment. We believe in the power of combining different perspectives and experiences to offer an amazing service to our customers.

We promise to bring you the best of locally sourced service with a personal touch.

Thanks in advance for doing business with us.

Our Services

At Rug Wrangler, we are dedicated to providing top-notch carpet cleaning, rug cleaning, and fabric furniture cleaning services for both residential and commercial clients. Our team uses the latest techniques and eco-friendly solutions to ensure your spaces are not just clean but truly revitalized. Explore our comprehensive list of services tailored to meet the needs of every client:

Residential Services

  • Carpet Cleaning
    Deep cleaning and stain removal to breathe new life into your carpets.
  • Rug Cleaning
    Specialized care for all types of rugs, ensuring they look their best and last longer.
  • Upholstery Cleaning
    Thorough cleaning of sofas, armchairs, and other fabric-covered furniture to remove dust, allergens, and stains.
  • Allergen Reduction
    Our cleaning process reduces common allergens from dust mites, pests, pollen, and pet dander to improve indoor air quality.

Commercial Services

  • Office Carpet Cleaning

  • Commercial Upholstery Cleaning

  • High-Traffic Area Cleaning
    Specialized services for areas with high foot traffic to remove dirt and reduce wear and tear.
  • Scheduled Maintenance Plans
    Customized cleaning schedules designed to meet the unique needs of your business, ensuring your premises are always clean and welcoming.
  • OPTION: Eco-Friendly Cleaning Solutions
    We use environmentally safe products for all our cleaning services, protecting your health and the planet.
